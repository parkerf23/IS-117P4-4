Superman

Born to the planet Krypton as Kal-El, later shot through the cosmose by his father to planet Earth after Krypton's destruction. Now an earthling known as Clark Kent, he is born with god-like abilities due to his Krytonian DNA and its affect from Earths yellow sun. With these new found powers, Clark dawns the mantle of Superman the protector of Earth and must now take on this heavy responsibility. His list of powers consists of solar energy, cosmic energy, soul energy absorbtion, superhuman strength, speed, agility, reflexs, stamina, senses, flight, heat vision, super flare, intersteller travel, invulnerability, and super-breath. Superman sounds like he is invincible, but he has a list of weaknesses such as kryptonite, magic, solar energy dependency, sensory overload, radiation poisoning, and power limitation. Of course with being such a powerful hero attracts a lot of attention from super-powered villains. But one villain stands out amongest the rest and that is Lex Luther, Superman's arch-enemy.

Wonder Woman

Born from the hands of Zeus himself on the island of Themyscira (Paradise Island). Before she took up the mantle of Wonder Woman, she is known as Diana Prince daughter to the Amazon's. She was sent out into mans world as an ambassador to the Amazonian people. She stands as a beacon of hope to all who find themselves in need. Her list of abilities consist of super strength, invulnerability, flight, combat skill, combat strategy, superhuman agility, healing factor, and magic weaponry. Her weaknesses consist of Bracelets of Submission, Lasso of Truth, firearms, blades, old Gods, dimensional travel, Bind of Veils, Scarecrow's Fear Gas, and poison. Now, Diana has a hefty roster of super-villains such as her sister Cheetah and Ares the God of War.

Green Lantern

Hal Jordan, selected by the gaurdains of the power battery of Will power was chosen to become the Green Lantern of Earth. As a young boy Hal has always dreamed of becoming a fighter pilot, it was until he was kicked out of the Air Force and came apon a crashed alien ship with a dying Green Lantern inside known as Abin Sur. Abin with his dying words entrusted Hal with the power of the Green Lantern core forever changing his life. Now gifted with such emense power, Hal is now the Green Lantern of sector 2814. With such power he is tasked with protecting Earth and his entire sector from impending threats and evil. Hal's abilities only stemp from what his imagination can create through the rings contructs, but also include aviation, indomitable will, leadership, hand-to-hand combat, and mechanical engineering. Now witht that being said, his list of weakness are as long as a normal humanbeings when he isnt wearing the ring. But when he is weilding his ring the speed force and power of the yellow ring of fear are his most powerful weakness's. Bieng a intergalactic police office he deffenitly has racked up a long list of enemies, but the two that stand out the most are Sinestro and Parallax.
