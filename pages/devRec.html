Animated Films:

Justic League The Flash Point Paradox

What if the Flash finally got the ability to travel through the speed force back in time to save his mother from being murdered. She was murdered by The Reverse Flash. Well, that day finally came, but what was the backlash of Berry saving his mommy? Berry awakes in a world similar to his own, but it's not. The world is on the brink of WW3, between the Amazons and the Atlantians. Whole countries were wiped from the map after being caught in the middle of this war. Now it's up to Berry to reclaim his abilities along with Batman (Thomas Wayne) to help save the world and correct the timeline before it all comes to an end.

Superman Doomsday

Superman is the symbol of hope and salvation to the people of Earth. No matter the threat, Superman is always there, but when a being of immense power known as Doomsday crashes to Earth and unleashes destruction upon Metropolis. Superman stands toe to toe with the beast, exchanging blows that shatter and shake the very ground around them. It seems he has met his match. Blow after blow causing Superman to bend to the monster's power, beaten and bloody, Clark makes a last-ditch effort to destroy the beast once and for all. But it comes at the cost of his own life in the process. Now, with a defeated Doomsday and the death of Earth's guardian, how will life go on?

Justice League Dark Apookolips War

It comes when our heroes least expect it. The Justice League is finally given an opportunity to take out Darkseid and Apokolips. Still, does it all seems too good to be true? As they begin to execute their plan, in the shadows, Darkseid smiles and laughs, knowing the entire plan from the start by tapping into Cyborg's operating system. The League falls right into his trap, and they won't be coming back this time. Darkseid unleashes his Paradooms that kill most of the League. Now that Darkseid has successfully conquered Earth, how will the remaining Justice League members save the planet?

Comic Arks:

Blackest Night

It always starts with the death of a giant, following the Final Crisis events and the passing of Batman. On a dark and gloomy night, a man by the name of Black Hand stands over the grave of Bruce Wayne. He pulls the skull of Bruce from the dirt in one and a black lantern power battery in the other. This is the beginning of the end. As the power battery begins to charge, a hoard of black lantern rings scatters across the stars. A voice calls out, "The dead shall rise." Once dead beings are brought back from the grave all across the universe, spreading death across the galaxy. The rings finally reach Earth, and once dead, heroes and villains are reanimated to do the black rings bidding. "John Jones of Mars rise," "Author Curry rise." Once reports of these rings reach Hal and Berry, they visit where it started at Bruce's grave only to be attacked and chased by black rings. They were able to get away from the rings and gather up other heroes and villains to figure out a solution to this now universal threat.

Dceased

It opens with the disappearance of Cyborg, kidnapped by Darkseid. Back on, Apokolips Darkseid has finally solved the anti-life equation and figured out that the other half of the equation has been inside of Victor. As he completes the anti-life equation, something calls outs. It drives the new god insane the infected Darkeid screams out in a blood-thirsty rage. He begins his rampage, finally leaping into the core of Apokolips to bring the planet to an end. At this moment, Victor boom tubes himself back to Earth but forgets one thing to shut his outgoing single off. It was too late. The corrupted anti-life equation spread through the signal reach to the farthest parts of the globe. It was infecting anyone in front of a screen, driving them into a murderous rage, turning Earth as we know it into a living hell. Now it is up to what's left of the League to figure out how to stop this rampaging techovirus.

Forever Evil

The events start to fall into place after the Apokolips War. The Multiverse barrier is weakened, allowing the Crime Syndicate to crawl its way into our hero's universe. They then devise a plan to cripple the Justice League and take over the planet. They use Deathstorm's ability to control the Firestorm Matrix to trap the League. After collecting villains from all over the world, the Crime Syndicate now has complete control of the planet. Who is to save the League and stop the Syndicate? It seems that it's up to Lex Luther, Batman, Catwoman, and a group of villains to save the Earth.

Video Games:

Batman Arkham Asylum

A dark and stormy night brings Batman hauling the Joker to Arkham Asylum. But something is off. It almost seems as if the Joker wanted to be caught. At that moment, the lights cut out, and the Joker lets out a psychotic laugh. The lights kicked back on, and he's made his escape into the Asylum. Joker then ceases control of the Asylum just as he planned to do. Now it is up to Batman to fight through a hoard of insane inmates, restore order to the Asylum, and bring Joker back behind bars.

Injustice Gods Amoung Us

In another universe the Joker has one final joke to tell and it isnt towards his best buddy Batman. He has devised a plan to combine Scarecrow's fear toxin with kryptonite and deploy it against the big blue boy scout himself Superman. At the same time Joker has hidden a nuke in the city of Metropolis all to bring Superman to his knees. The events fall into motion, first baiting Clark by kiddnaping Lois Lane and dousing him in the kryptonite feartoxin mixture causing him to see things that arent really their. All of a sudden Doomsday appears and the battle begins. Clark not having the time or patient grabs the monster and rockets him to the uppermost atmosphere. At that time Clark snaps out of the effects of the concoction and hears the sounds of two hearts stop. Noticing that at that moment he had just killed Lois and thier unborn child, then triggering the switch to Jokers nuke which was linked to Lois's and the babys heartbeat. The Joker has broken The Man of Steel, driving him to break his moral code. In a fit of rage Clark kills the Joker, setting him on his new path of the Regiem.
