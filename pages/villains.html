Darksied

Deemed a new God, by his own hands and the ruler of Apokolips is a major villain of the DC universe and on countless times has brought the entire universe to its knees. The mighty ruler of the hellscape planet known as Apokoplis, he is a merciless tyrant who demanded unwavering devotion and adject fear from all his subjects. Darksieds driven by his obsession of finding and understanding the Anti-Life equation. A universal msytery scource to controlling all life in the known universe becasue in the end all will kneel to Darksied. This mighty god's abilities seem to have no limits consisting of superhuman strength, stamina, and durability, genius-level intelligence, flight, telekinesis, telepathy, matter and energy manipulation, immortality, invulnerability, dimensional travel, and omega effect. Most of the entire universe stands agenst this mad new god, but his mortal enemy is the last son of Krypton Superman.

Deathstroke

Feared throughout the entire world as the the most deadly mercenary. Deathstroke aka Slade Wilson is a killer higher that has brought even the Justic League to its knees and made gods beg for their lives at his feet. Slade agreed to undergo an experimental process that increased his mental and physical abilities to a superhuman level. Even though being a rutherless contract killer, Deathstroke from time to time has helped protect the world and in his own mind followed a moral code when taking contracts. He can be identified by his signature red and blue mercenary armor wielding two deadly katanas. Included with his superhuman strength are a vast amount of other abilities including exceptional martial arts abilities, combat strategy, durability, enhanced strength and speed, weapons expert, superhuman agility, master swordsman, and an incredible healing factor. As stated, Slade has gone toe to toe with the best of them including Batman from time to time, but not as mush as the Teen Titans. On multiple occassions Slade has waged war on the Titans for his own personal reasons.
