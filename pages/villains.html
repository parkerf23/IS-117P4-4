The Source:

It was the literal beginning of all created throughout the universe. It was said to have been created over 19 billion years ago and is located at the edge of the known multiverse. Here, the Source Wall protects the Source by trapping all who try and pass through. This cosmic energy is the supposed beginning of the Godwave that embued the Gods with their divine abilities.

Darkseid

Deemed a new God, by his own hands and the ruler of Apokolips is a major villain of the DC universe and has brought the entire universe to its knees countless times. The mighty ruler of the hellscape planet, Darkseid, is a ruthless tyrant demanding unwavering devotion and adjected fear from all his subjects. Darkseid's driven by his obsession with finding and understanding the Anti-Life equation. A universal mystery source to controlling all life in the known universe because, in the end, all will kneel to Darkseid. This mighty god's abilities seem to have no limits consisting of superhuman strength, stamina, and durability, genius-level intelligence, flight, telekinesis, telepathy, matter and energy manipulation, immortality, invulnerability, dimensional travel, and omega effect. Most of the entire universe stands against this mad new god, but his mortal enemy is the last son of Krypton Superman.

Anti Monitor

This massive being is known other than the Anit-Monitor, who is the controller of the Antimatter Universe. During the Crisis on Infinite Earths world-ending event, this being almost succeeded in destroying the positive matter Multiverse, killing a plethora of beings in the process. Also known by many as the Anti-God, he possesses untold abilities including super strength, stamina, and durability, enhanced senses, anti-matter manipulation, cosmic powers, anti-matter blast, reality-warping, immortality, invulnerability, size manipulation, dimensional travel, genius-level intellect, absorption of the entire universe, power distribution, energy manipulation, absorption, and construct creation. The Anit-God is not only the mortal enemy of the Monitor but the whole of the positive universe itself.

Deathstroke

He was feared throughout the entire world as the most deadly mercenary. Deathstroke, aka Slade Wilson, is a killer higher that has brought even the Justice League to its knees and made gods beg for their lives at his feet. Slade agreed to undergo an experimental process that increased his mental and physical abilities to a superhuman level. Even though being a ruthless contract killer, Deathstroke, from time to time, has helped protect the world and, in his mind, followed a moral code when taking contracts. He can be identified by his signature red and blue mercenary armor wielding two deadly katanas. His superhuman strength includes a vast amount of other abilities, including exceptional martial arts abilities, combat strategy, durability, enhanced strength and speed, weapons expert, superhuman agility, master swordsman, and an incredible healing factor. As stated, Slade has gone toe to toe with the best of them, including Batman from time to time, but not as much as the Teen Titans. Slade has waged war on the Titans for his own personal reasons on multiple occasions.

Black Adam

Chosen as the original wielder of the Wizard Shazams powers, Teth-Adam was an ancient Egyptian slave. Dawning the name Black Adam, he took up the mantle of the Wizard. Black Adam was imprisoned for millennia by the same Wizard for misuse of his powers. Throughout history, his heroic actions include liberating his home of Kahndaq from its dictators and the Seven Sins. His banishment was due to his revolt against the Council of Eternity, murdering all except the Wizard Shazam, who then cast Adam to his imprisonment. Adam has a list of abilities that rival even the strongest heroes of the DC universe, including divine empowerment, the stamina of Shu, self-sustenance, speed of Horus, flight, superhuman agility, reflexes, the strength of Amun, wisdom of Zehuti, enhanced senses, power of Aten, electrokinesis, the courage of Mehen, accelerated healing, immortality, and magic being such a powerhouse Black Adam as has his weaknesses such as his abilities being nullified and his inability to revert to his mortal form without fading into dust. Adam's must-hatted foe is the hero Shazam aka Billy Baston. He is the current wielder of the Wizard Shazams powers.
