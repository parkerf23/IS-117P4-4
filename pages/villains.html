Darksied

Deemed a new God, by his own hands and the ruler of Apokolips is a major villain of the DC universe and on countless times has brought the entire universe to its knees. The mighty ruler of the hellscape planet known as Apokoplis, he is a merciless tyrant who demanded unwavering devotion and adject fear from all his subjects. Darksieds driven by his obsession of finding and understanding the Anti-Life equation. A universal msytery scource to controlling all life in the known universe becasue in the end all will kneel to Darksied. This mighty god's abilities seem to have no limits consisting of superhuman strength, stamina, and durability, genius-level intelligence, flight, telekinesis, telepathy, matter and energy manipulation, immortality, invulnerability, dimensional travel, and omega effect. Most of the entire universe stands agenst this mad new god, but his mortal enemy is the last son of Krypton Superman.
